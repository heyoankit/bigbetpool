👤 Player Experience

Step 1 — Integration via webhook

When a player places a bet, your backend sends a POST to our endpoint:

POST https://api.bigbetpool.io/v1/bet-placed
{"operator_id":"your-op-id","user_id":"player-uid","bet_amount":100.00}

Step 2 — Draw configuration

  • Draw frequency — daily, weekly, or monthly with date ranges
  • Prize pool — fixed or % of total bets
  • Winners and prize distribution
  • Recurring or one-time draw

Step 3 — Winner notification

Your platform receives results via webhook:

POST https://your-platform.com/bbp-webhook
{"winners":[{"user_id":"uid_1234","prize":2500,"rank":1}]}

Step 4 — Social media reveal

Record the Draw Simulator reveal screen for social media — winners appear one by one for maximum suspense.
